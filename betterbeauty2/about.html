ABOUT US

Our mission is to filter beauty products that are clean, vegan, and cruelty-free. Many products focus more on advertising the benefits rather than actually working towards a clean beauty product. The products featured on our site are certified and approved without any harmful ingredients. Every person should be provided clean options that work for them.

In the US, less that 5% of makeup and cosmetics is vegan and cruelty-free. Many consumers believe that products that test on animals are cheaper, however it is because vegan brands are less popular - not necessarily more expensive. By increasing the awarness about these products, users can use better products for less.
